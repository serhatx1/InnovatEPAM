# EPIC-03: Evaluation Workflow

## Goal
Enable admin/evaluator users to review ideas and record final decisions.

## Scope
- Status transitions (`under_review`, `accepted`, `rejected`)
- Decision comments
- Enforce comment on rejection
- Decision visibility on idea detail

## Related Story
- US3: Review & Decide Ideas (`stories/US3-review-decide-ideas.md`)

## Related Tasks
- Task list: `EPIC-03-tasks.md`

## Acceptance Summary
- Admin can update idea status and save comments.
- Rejection requires comment.
- Submitter can view final status and evaluator comment.
